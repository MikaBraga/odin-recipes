Brigadeiro



Ingredients


  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 395 g sweetened condensed milk
  • 30 g cocoa powder
  • 1 cup chocolate sprinkle - as needed


Method

  2. Cook the mixture for about 10 minutes over medium-low heat, until thick, fudgy, and itâ€™s coming off from the bottom of the pot. If you tilt the pan over, the whole mixture should come off, as if it was falling in one piece
